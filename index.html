Somsa

Somsa is a popular savory pastry originating from Uzbekistan and widely enjoyed across Central Asia. It consists of a delicate, flaky dough that encases a flavorful filling typically made from minced meat, often lamb, along with finely chopped onions and a blend of aromatic spices. The meat mixture is carefully wrapped in the dough and then baked or fried until the pastry turns golden brown and achieves a satisfyingly crispy texture.

Osh / Palov

Palov(Osh) is a traditional rice dish from Uzbekistan, featuring a flavorful blend of rice, meat (often lamb or beef), carrots, onions, and aromatic spices. It's cooked in a special pot called a "kazan" and holds cultural importance in Central Asian cuisine, often served at celebrations and gatherings.

$20.99 per 1 kg

Norin

Norin, also known as "naryn," is a traditional Central Asian cold noodle dish originating from Uzbekistan. It consists of thin, flat noodles made from dough, usually served with finely sliced pieces of boiled meat (often beef or horse meat), along with ingredients like radishes, tomatoes, and onions. The dish is typically dressed with a tangy, spiced vinegar-based sauce and can be garnished with herbs and spices for added flavor.

$18.99 per 1 kg

Qazi

Qazi, also known as "kazi," is a traditional cured meat sausage that holds cultural significance in Uzbek cuisine. It's made from horse meat, which is seasoned with various spices, such as garlic, black pepper, and sometimes paprika, before being stuffed into casings and air-dried. Qazi has a distinctive and intense flavor due to the curing process and the combination of spices used. It's often enjoyed as a special delicacy during festive occasions and gatherings in Uzbekistan.

$25.99 a piece

Shashlik

Shashlik is a popular grilled meat dish that originates from the Middle East and Central Asia, including countries like Uzbekistan, Russia, and Pakistan. It typically consists of skewered and marinated chunks of meat, often lamb, beef, or chicken, although other types of meat can also be used. The meat is usually marinated with a mixture of spices, herbs, and sometimes yogurt or citrus juices to enhance its flavor and tenderness.

$8.99 a piece
